# Why Adelaide Small Businesses Are Losing Customers After Hours

Every missed call is a missed opportunity. For a hair salon, a tradie, or a local clinic in Adelaide, that missed call might be a $300 booking walking straight to a competitor.

Research consistently shows that **30–40% of calls to small businesses go unanswered**. The reasons are understandable — you're busy with customers in front of you, it's outside business hours, or your team is at capacity. But from the caller's perspective, an unanswered phone means moving on.

## The After-Hours Problem

Adelaide businesses operate in a competitive market. Customers don't just call during business hours anymore — they call when they think of it: Sunday evenings, early mornings, during lunch breaks.

If your business isn't available then, someone else's is.

A traditional solution — hiring a receptionist or answering service — costs $40,000–$60,000 per year for a full-time employee, or $200–$500/month for a basic call answering service that just takes a message.
